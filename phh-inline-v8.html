About PHH

Craig was an owner first. That changes everything.

Professional Holiday Homes was founded by Craig Reid, who understood the frustration of managing a holiday home from afar — the inconsistent income, the unreliable managers, the sleepless nights when something went wrong.

Before launching PHH, Craig ran his own properties on the Jervis Bay coastline and won national industry awards for guest experience. When he couldn't find a manager he trusted, he built one — and set out to create the kind of service he'd always wanted as an owner.

Today, PHH manages 200+ properties across the NSW South Coast, from Hyams Beach to Sussex Inlet. As a fully licensed NSW real estate agency with statutory trust accounting, we operate with a level of financial rigour and legal accountability that sets us apart from app-based competitors who are simply platforms.

We're also the only local specialist agency in the Jervis Bay area to use PriceLabs for dynamic pricing — because local knowledge alone isn't enough. You need both: the data and the local expertise to interpret it.

We inspect the property after every stay and photograph anything that appears damaged or missing. We then pursue resolution through the platform (Airbnb or Stayz resolution centres) and our own processes on your behalf. We also work with your home insurance and can advise on specialist short-term rental insurance policies that provide more comprehensive protection than standard home cover.

As a licensed NSW real estate agency, PHH is legally required to hold owner funds in statutory trust accounts — separate from our operating accounts. This means your rental income is protected even if something happened to our business. You receive monthly statements showing every booking, every deduction, and every deposit. It's the same standard that licensed real estate agencies use for long-term rental management, and it's the gold standard of financial transparency in property management. Most Airbnb-model management companies are not bound by this obligation.
